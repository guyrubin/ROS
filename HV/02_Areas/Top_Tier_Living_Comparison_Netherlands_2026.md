**Best for:** prestige today, international network, schools/culture, old-money positioning.  
**Weakness:** small/old stock, parking, children+car friction, lower upside because premium already priced.

**Best listing fit:** Stadionkade 27-3.

### Amsterdam Noord — next-generation affluent

**Best for:** modern family life, parking sanity, value/upside, tech/expat ecosystem, future wealth positioning.  
**Weakness:** less legacy prestige today; some areas remain transitional.

**Best listing fit:** Badhuiskade 217.  
Why: modern, A+++, 111 m², strong amenities, views, Overhoeks, low maintenance, and still below top Oud-Zuid renovated pricing per m².

**Best development fit:** Asterweg 19 / Nieuwendammerdijk 536 / Vlierweg-type Buiksloterham BOG assets.

### Scheveningen / Statenkwartier / Belgisch Park — coastal prestige

**Best for:** calm, diplomacy/embassy ecosystem, beach, larger houses, family comfort, car.  
**Weakness:** weaker Amsterdam career-density, less tech/founder energy, likely lower upside than Noord.

## 5. Risks / red flags

### Data risks

- Funda direct pages were anti-bot blocked in this environment. I used MVA/MarketSuite public listing API for Amsterdam address-level details and public search snippets for Funda/market context.
- Kadaster address-level sold comparables were not pulled. Before offer, buy Kadaster koopsommen / reference transactions for the exact street and 500m radius.
- Rental numbers are market proxies from Huure pages. They are not WWS-compliant rent opinions.

### Asset risks

- **Amsterdam Zuid older stock:** foundation, roof, VvE reserves, monument/protected cityscape, energy upgrade cost.
- **Noord high-rise/new-build:** service charges, leasehold/own-land details, VvE rules, parking rights, short-term rental restrictions.
- **BOG/commercial assets:** financing, zoning, conversion permits, fire safety, BBL, VAT/transfer tax, whether residential use is allowed.
- **Hague coastal:** maintenance from sea air, older villas, protected streetscape, lower career-density.

### Hold

Hold on Scheveningen/Statenkwartier unless you decide the primary objective is calm coastal family life rather than Amsterdam ecosystem/upside.

### Do not proceed yet

Do not buy any BOG/commercial asset for living before confirming:

- zoning plan and permitted use;
- VvE/split-deed restrictions;
- municipality conversion policy;
- lender treatment;
- transfer tax/VAT treatment;
- fire/BBL compliance;
- whether residential value after conversion justifies all-in cost.
