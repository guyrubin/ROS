- Energy label C: upside exists, but cost and VvE approval may be material.
- Third/fourth floor family living: check stroller/kids practicality and lift absence.
- Parking is likely difficult versus Noord.
- Permit/VvE dependency if changing roof, terrace, structural walls, or insulation envelope.
