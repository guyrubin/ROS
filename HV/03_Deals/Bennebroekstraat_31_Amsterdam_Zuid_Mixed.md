## Risks / red flags

- Status is pending offer; may not be actionable.
- Legal residential status of dwelling/studio must be verified.
- Rental contracts for bike/motor storage must be reviewed.
- VAT/transfer tax treatment may be complex because of mixed residential/BOG use.
- VvE must allow intended use and any future conversion.
- Fire safety and access path shared with stalling users may reduce family-living quality.

## Financial impact

- Attractive €/m² versus Amsterdam Zuid residential values, but this is because most of the area is BOG/storage.
- Income provides partial carry, but not enough alone to justify investor pricing unless conversion/use upside is real.
- Could be powerful if the dwelling/studio can be improved while maintaining income.
