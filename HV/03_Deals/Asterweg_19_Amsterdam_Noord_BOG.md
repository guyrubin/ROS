# Asterweg 19 — Amsterdam Noord BOG

## Executive takeaway

**Recommendation: Proceed only as development-potential DD, not as assumed personal residence.**  
This is the cleanest small-ticket Amsterdam Noord business-space candidate from the scan: 187 m², €625k, label A, in a mixed living/working growth area. However, residential use/conversion is **not confirmed** and is the critical path.

## Facts

- Ask: **€625,000**
- Area: **187 m²**
- Price per m²: **~€3,342/m²**
- Type: bedrijfsruimte / kantoorruimte
- Energy label: **A**
- Location: Asterweg, Amsterdam Noord
- Listing status at source check: **Beschikbaar**
- Source: MVA public BOG listing API / listing page checked 2026-05-15

## Listing evidence

Listing text states:

- office space of approx. 187 m²;
- located in Amsterdam Noord;
- area is “volop in ontwikkeling” and popular because of combination of living and working;
- sixth floor with IJ views;
- includes pantry, two office rooms and storage room;
- area contains companies such as Joolz, &Klevering, Basic-Fit, Shell Technology Centre, Eye and scale-ups/fashion companies.

## Assumptions

- Primary upside is not current office yield; it is optionality:
  - live/work use;
  - future conversion;
  - area appreciation;
  - scarcity of affordable large square meters close to Overhoeks/Buiksloterham.

## Risks / red flags

- **Major:** cannot assume legal residential use.
- Must check bestemmingsplan/omgevingsplan, VvE/split deed, BBL, fire separation, ventilation, daylight, escape route and municipality policy.
- Financing may be BOG, not residential mortgage.
- Transfer tax/VAT treatment may differ from residential owner-occupier.
- Sixth-floor office conversion may be technically constrained.

## Financial impact

- Entry €/m² is far below residential Overhoeks/Noord values, but this discount exists because it is BOG.
- Conversion value only exists if legal path is real.
- If residential conversion is impossible, it remains a small office investment / own workspace, not a family-living solution.

## Recommendation

**Proceed if conditions met.**

Proceed only if:

1. Municipality confirms residential or live/work potential.
2. VvE/split deed allows use change.
3. Financing can be structured acceptably.
4. All-in cost after conversion remains below comparable residential value.
5. Exit/liquidity is clear.

- Ask lender/broker about financing as BOG vs mixed-use.
