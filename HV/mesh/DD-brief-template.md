# HV Due-Diligence Research Brief — template

**The HV deal mesh's research lane (ROS-BACKLOG D2).** `hv-orchestrator` / `hv-sourcing` fills the **Ask** and dispatches **`research-agent`** (KK-owned shared service); research-agent returns the **Brief** below, sourced and verified; HV owns the synthesis into the IC memo (the muscle is HV's, the research capability stays shared). Copy this per deal into `HV/03_Deals/<deal>/DD-research.md`.

---

## Ask (HV fills before dispatch)
- **Deal / address:** …
- **Strategy + thesis:** BRRRR / value-add — the upside driver to verify (rent uplift / unit add / sqm add / label / refi arbitrage)
- **Specific questions:** … (e.g. "is splitting to 2 units zoning-feasible?", "realistic post-reno rent for 75m²?")

## Brief (research-agent returns — every claim cited)
1. **Comparables** — recent **sales** + **rent** comps within the micro-area (€/m², date, source). The basis for ARV and stabilised rent.
2. **Area trajectory** — development pipeline, demand drivers, transport/amenity changes, gentrification signal; direction over a 3–5y horizon.
3. **Regulation & WWS** — rent ceiling / liberalisation threshold (Wet betaalbare huur / WWS), `bestemmingsplan` zoning fit for the intended use/split, monument / protected-cityscape constraints, recent policy shifts.
4. **Vendor / counterparty signal** — reputation of any named agent / notary / contractor (only if named); red flags.
5. **Risk flags** — anything that could break the thesis; unknowns that need a site visit or a specialist.
6. **Confidence & sources** — confidence per section + the source list; mark anything unverified.

## Gate (per UNIVERSAL-LOOP Research DoD + HV DoD)
Every material claim cited · key claims triangulated · uncertainty flagged · synthesized (not a link dump). HV then folds this into the IC memo with facts/assumptions/risks separated.
