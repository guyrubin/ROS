## Smart scoring model

Score each new listing 1–5 against:

1. **Family quality** — bedrooms, layout, lift/stairs, storage, outdoor space.
2. **Car sanity** — parking, garage, permit pressure, EV potential.
3. **International ecosystem** — proximity to Amsterdam business, schools, expats, airports/train.
4. **Lifestyle** — restaurants, culture, beach/water/parks, walkability.
5. **Future upside** — area transformation, €/m² discount, energy upgrade, layout/sqm/addition potential.
6. **Development optionality** — live/work, BOG conversion, split/add unit, roof/extension, mixed-use.
7. **Execution risk** — monument, foundation, VvE, zoning, BBL/fire, financing, leasehold.

## Auto-watch criteria for better listings

A new listing is worth flagging if it meets one of these:

- Amsterdam Noord residential: **≥90 m²**, **2+ bedrooms**, energy **A or better**, ask under **€1.1m**, or **<€8,250/m²** in Overhoeks/Buiksloterham.
- Amsterdam Noord family/new-build: **3 bedrooms**, parking/own land/low VvE, under **€750k**.
- Amsterdam Zuid prestige: **≥130 m²**, **3+ bedrooms**, under **€9,000/m²**, or obvious renovation upside.
- Amsterdam Zuid garden/family: ground-floor + garden + 3+ bedrooms under **€2.0m**.
- BOG / development: under **€3,500/m²** in Noord/Zuid with plausible live-work/conversion signal.
- Den Haag coastal: Statenkwartier/Belgisch Park family house with parking/outdoor space and unusually good €/m².
