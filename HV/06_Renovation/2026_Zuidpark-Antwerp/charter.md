# Project Charter — Zuidpark, Antwerpen (PRE-ACQUISITION SCAFFOLD)

It exists now to (a) prove the PM capability and (b) make the baseline explicit before any bid.

## 1. Scope & concept
- **What we'd build:** split the whole GLV+4 block into **4–5 stacked units** (one per level) — full gut; optopping only if heritage roofline permits (do **not** bank it). Park/Scheldt/KMSKA frontage is the value.
- **Net sellable area:** ~290–310 m² net (385 m² gross − 20–25% on a narrow stacked plan with new code stair/fire lobby — per hv-architecture).
- **Exit:** develop-to-sell the units (BE: 21% VAT on transformed units sold to investor — model it). BRRRR-hold is weak (Antwerp rent ~€10–11/m²/mo) → sell is the primary exit.
