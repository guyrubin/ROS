## A. Get Arvo out of the chat
The Arvo avatar rendered in chat is downloadable from the widget (hover → download → `arvo_arbor_avatar_presenter`). Save as PNG 1080×1080 = channel profile picture + end-card mark.

> **Speaker choice (read this):** HeyGen lip-syncs *realistic* faces best; a cartoon mascot won't animate cleanly. So the **speaker** = a warm HeyGen avatar (pick a calm parent-aged "instant avatar," or record a custom one of yourself / a parent). **Arvo** = the brand: channel pic, the end card, and a lower-third logo. Best of both — a human voice with Arvo as the mark.

---

## B. HeyGen setup
- **Video:** new project, **9:16 portrait**, ~50s.
- **Avatar:** a calm, warm instant avatar (or your custom one).
- **Voice (EN):** a calm, warm natural voice; **pace ~0.9×**; tone = unhurried, kind, certain. Add small pauses at the line breaks.
- **Voice (HE):** HeyGen multilingual → a calm Hebrew female/male voice; same pacing. Render a second HE version.
- **Captions:** turn ON burned-in captions (most watch muted).
- **Music:** add soft, sparse track at ~15% volume; let it resolve to calm at Scene 4.

## C. Scene-by-scene script (paste one scene per HeyGen scene)

> **Format per scene:** *Spoken* = the avatar script box · *Overlay* = the text element you add · *Background* = media/B-roll behind the avatar.
