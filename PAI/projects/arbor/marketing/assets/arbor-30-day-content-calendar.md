**Owner:** arbor-marketing-lead
**Created:** 2026-06-21
**Market:** Israel-first (HE primary), EN parallel where noted

---

## Structural rules (honor before reading the day-by-day)

1. **WhatsApp Tier 1 = already-trusted group members.** Recruited at Day -21. Their action in launch week: share their own child's genuine Hero Comic as a personal parent moment. No promo caption. Referral code lives silently in the image deep-link. This is NOT a marketing drop — it is a real parent moment that happens to carry a code. Do not brief them to "promote" anything.
2. **AEO is a 60-day channel.** All AEO/SEO work ships and gets indexed as fast as possible, but the 30-day registration model is sourced 100% from creator/WhatsApp/paid. Do not count organic search registrations in the 30-day plan.
3. **Day-7 K kill gate is non-negotiable.** If K < 0.3 by end of day 7: hold all paid, diagnose share friction, do not spend EUR 3k behind a broken loop.
4. **Paid amplification = Level 4.** No paid spend executes without explicit confirmation from Guy/CoS routed through the orchestrator with amounts stated. Day-7 gate must clear first.
5. **Hebrew is native voice, not translation.** All HE copy passes native reviewer before external publish (Level 3).
6. **Brand-voice rule per day:** one emotional register per post. No mixing delight + clinical in the same caption.

## Launch Week — Days 1–7 (Day-by-Day)

### Day 1 — Launch
**Channels active:** IG / TikTok / YouTube Shorts (HE) + WhatsApp Tier 1 + PR

**AM (creators post, 09:00–13:00 IL time — coordinated 4-hour window):**
- 15+ IL micro-creators post their Hero Comic reveal Reels simultaneously. This is the saturation mechanic. No single creator should feel like an ad; the simultaneous posting reads as "everyone's using this."
- Caption format: one of the five ready-to-paste options from the creator brief. Delight tone or "this is so us" tone preferred for Day 1. No clinical language.
- Hashtag: #הגיבורשלי on every post.

**Morning (Arbor brand accounts, 09:30):**
- IG Story: manifesto headline "לא פחות — הורות שמחזיקה" with link to the full manifesto page (if published) or email sign-up. Sage-paper + emerald clay visual. Fraunces display type on image.
- Facebook IL parenting groups: Arbor account posts the manifesto as a genuine thought piece — not as a product ad. No CTA in the body. Product mention in comments only if organically asked.

**Afternoon (Tier 1 WhatsApp):**
- Ambassadors share their child's Hero Comic in their class/gan groups naturally, as a parent moment. Example authentic framing (this is NOT a script — ambassadors share in their own voice): "הנה ה[שם]..." with the comic image. The referral deep-link is in the image. No further action required.

**Evening (Arbor IG/TikTok — brand):**
- Arbor account reposts 2–3 strongest creator Reels to IG Stories with @creator tag. No additional copy.

**PR goal:** Embargo lift today. Calcalist / Geektime story live if reporter filed. If not, follow up with key stat: registrations on Day 1.

**Morning K read:** Record baseline. Day 1 K is usually low (sharing is just starting). No action if K < 0.3 today — that gate applies at Day 7.

## Scale Phase — Days 8–30

### Days 8–14 — Paid Amplification (Level 4 gate)
**Trigger:** Day-7 K gate cleared + Guy/CoS Level 4 confirmation received.

**Day 8:** Launch Meta IL campaign. Budget: EUR 2,200. Structure: Advantage+ audience (broad, IL, Hebrew-language, parents ages 26–42) + one retargeting set (engaged with creator content in prior 7 days). Creatives: the 2–3 organic creator videos with the highest engagement and share rate from Days 1–7 only. No new untested creatives in paid.

**Day 9:** Launch TikTok IL campaign. Budget: EUR 500. Same 2–3 winning creatives. TopView not needed; In-Feed only.

**Day 10:** Retargeting. Budget: EUR 300. Target: landing page visitors (Days 1–9) who did not register. Creative: the "2am answer" variant (parent worry hook, not comic hook). A/B: comic variant vs 2am variant.

**Daily from Days 8–14:** Read CAC every morning. Kill any ad set at EUR 60 spend with zero registrations. If blended CAC rises above EUR 80: pause the channel, report to orchestrator.

**Content (organic, parallel to paid):**
- Days 8–10: Rhythm sub-challenge launch. "Screenshot the meltdown you called it" — parents who have now used Arbor for 7 days may have early Rhythm data. Ask them to share prediction vs actual. This is the analytical-parent viral hook. Creative: "ארבור ניבא את ההתפרצות השעה 17:00 — ובדיוק זה קרה."
- Day 12: "Ask a Specialist" education post — what it is, why the expert not starting cold matters. Non-clinical, parent-voice.
- Day 14: Lifecycle email 5 (Daily Play intro) + check founding-member paywall state.

### Days 15–21 — Creator Wave 2 + Referral Push

**Day 15:** Creator wave 2 saturates. Target: 40 total unique IL creators have posted by end of Day 15. Arbor account creates a creator highlight reel — "40 הורים ישראלים. אחד ארבור" — published as IG Reel and YouTube Short.

**Day 16:** Post-activation referral prompts go live (P2-7). Three in-product prompts at delight peaks: post-comic, post-Rhythm-hit, post-coach-win. Each with one-tap WhatsApp share + pre-written HE caption with referral code embedded. This is the K-floor mechanism: in-product prompts sustain K after the organic launch wave fades.

**Day 17:** WhatsApp Tier 2 ambassadors now 10+ days active in their groups. They can now naturally mention Arbor when relevant — not as a promotion, but in response to questions they encounter in the group (developmental questions, "what do you do when X" questions).

**Day 18:** Practice Studio education Reel. Shows a real speech exercise from the app. Caption: "הפרקטיקה שנבחרה בשביל השלב של הילד שלך — לא רשימה כללית." Ends with "הילד שלך. הסיפור שלו."

**Day 19:** Lifecycle email 6 (founding-member Plus offer if gate is cleared). If 50+ reviews: "EUR 89/yr founding member — [N] spots left." If not yet 50 reviews: "14 days free — here's what Plus parents said this week."

**Day 21:** Second K-factor read. If K < 0.3 still: reduce paid spend, double down on share-friction diagnosis. If K >= 0.4: surface to Guy/CoS for possible budget increase from the EUR 3.6k reserve.

### Days 22–30 — Scarcity Close + NL Teaser

**Day 22:** Scarcity close sequence begins (if founding-member gate cleared). In-app banner: "ימי המשפחות המייסדות מסתיימים ביום ראשון — [N] מקומות נותרו." Email (from lifecycle arc, day 22 variant): "המקומות אוזלים." Tone: calm, factual. Not pressure.

**Day 24:** Second PR beat. Pitch IL tech press with actual numbers: registrations, K-factor, creator results. Story angle: "calm AI in a fear-selling category, built for Israeli parents, zero diagnostic claims." If a reporter ran the Day 1 story, this is the follow-up with real data.

**Day 25:** Lifecycle email (day 25 variant): "here's what Plus parents said this week." Pure parent testimonials (real quotes from actual users by now). No selling — social proof is the conversion mechanism.

**Day 26:** Growth Card preview post. Arbor brand shows what a monthly Growth Card looks like for a child who has been using Arbor for 25 days. Show the sage-paper field-notebook aesthetic. Caption: "25 יום. הרגלים. הפתעות. הרגעים שלא שכחת לרשום — ועכשיו הם כאן." CTA: "Plus families receive this on the 1st of every month."

**Day 28:** NL/English teaser. One EN-language Reel: top-performing IL creator comic, subtitled in English, with "Coming to the Netherlands this summer. Join the waitlist." Link to NL waitlist landing (simple email capture, no product access yet). Target: 200 NL waitlist registrants.

**Day 29:** Final scarcity close. Email (day 29 variant): "tonight is the last night for founding-member pricing." In-app banner. Social posts (IG + Facebook). Do not panic-sell; state the fact: tonight is the cutoff.

**Day 30:** Post-launch summary. Internal: write the performance report against all KPIs (K-factor / activation / aha→share / free→paid / CAC). Surface to PAI/CoS via orchestrator. Memory entry updated. Creator program review: which creators drove the most activations (not just registrations)? Extend affiliate relationships with the top 10.

## Key adversarial fixes honored in this calendar

1. **WhatsApp Tier 1 = Day -21 recruit, not Day -7.** Ambassadors have weeks of natural presence before sharing. Their Day 1 action is a genuine parent moment, not a promo drop. Tier 2 ambassadors do not mention Arbor until Day 17 (10+ days active).
2. **AEO is a 60-day channel.** The AEO hub is deployed at Day -10 for indexing, but no 30-day registration targets come from organic search. The calendar sources 100% of 30-day registrations from creator/WhatsApp/paid.
3. **Double Aha cold-start honest framing.** The Rhythm sub-challenge (Days 8–10) invites users who have been on the platform for 7+ days — not Day 1 registrants. The Day 3 Rhythm creative is framed as aspirational, not as a current product claim for new users.
4. **Founding-member paywall gate.** The EUR 89/yr founding offer does not activate in the calendar until the 50+ reviews gate clears (tracked Day 7 and Day 14). Before that: 14-day no-card trial only.
5. **Day-7 K kill gate is day-by-day explicit.** Paid spend is a separate budget line, Level 4 gated, sequenced after Day 7 gate clearance.
