# Arbor IL — Ambassador Recruitment Pack + Group Seeding Playbook

**Owner:** `arbor-distribution`
**Gate:** Level 3 — all outbound messages (recruitment DMs, group posts, ambassador briefs) are drafts only. Require explicit confirmation from `arbor-marketing-lead` or Guy before sending.
**Safety:** no real child face/voice/data as a payload. No diagnostic claim. No clinical language.
**Seeding rule:** every group post is written native for that group's register. Never paste the same copy across groups. Value-first; link in first comment, never the opening post.

---

## Ambassador criteria (the 5–10 trusted parents)

The ambassador is not a creator, not an influencer. They are a real IL parent already active in the communities we need to reach — someone whose word carries social weight in their class WhatsApp group, neighborhood Facebook group, or parenting community. Their job is to share their genuine experience. There is no posting requirement attached to the free Family plan.

Select ambassadors who meet ALL of the following:

1. **Already active in the target communities.** They are a known, trusted voice in at least one WhatsApp class-group (of 20+ parents) or a large Facebook IL parenting group. Not a lurker — a person who answers questions, gives advice, shares their experience.
2. **Genuine parenting context.** They have at least one child aged 0–10 and are actively navigating a real parenting challenge (sleep, behavior, speech, school transition — any of the six surfaces applies).
3. **No competing product affiliation.** They are not currently posting sponsored content for Kinedu, Huckleberry, BabySparks, or any direct competitor.
4. **Brand-safe.** Their existing posts are warm, honest, non-political in the child/parenting domain. They do not fear-sell parenting anxiety to their audience.
5. **Willing to try the product with no strings.** The offer is free Family access. They are not required to post. If they love it and post organically, excellent. If they don't, that is also fine.

Sourcing approach: ambassadors come from the same sourcing pass as Tier C creators but with a different ask. Look for active parents answering questions in the Facebook groups we plan to seed — identify them from the group, then DM on IG or WhatsApp, not inside the group.

## WhatsApp class-group seeding playbook

### The mechanics of the engine

WhatsApp class-groups (קבוצות כיתה) are 20–80 IL parents. They are intimate, high-trust, and extremely fast-spreading — a good share here travels faster than any IG post. They also have zero tolerance for spam; a brand link dropped cold gets the parent removed from the group and Arbor associated with spam.

The engine works through ambassadors only. An ambassador shares their genuine experience in a group they belong to — not us posting from the outside. This is non-negotiable.

### Ambassador group post scripts (HE)

**DRAFT STATUS — L3 GATED. Ambassadors use these as a starting point only; they must rewrite in their own voice. Do not send verbatim scripts to groups.**

These are starting-point templates. The ambassador must adapt every post to sound like them. Send the template with this instruction explicitly stated.

**Hard rules for WhatsApp seeding:**
- Value comes before the link, every time. If the ambassador opens with a link they will be flagged and the group relationship is burned.
- Never claim "many parents in this group use Arbor" unless it is verified true.
- Never ask the ambassador to seed more than one WhatsApp group in the same 48-hour window. Spreading too fast reads as coordinated, not organic.
- The ambassador does not represent Arbor in the group — they share their personal experience.

## Facebook IL parent mega-group seeding playbook

### Target groups

IL Facebook parent mega-groups have tens of thousands of members. They are moderated; admin rules vary. Most remove obvious commercial links in opening posts. Common groups to target:

- "אמהות" mega-groups (search: "אמהות ישראל", "אמהות וילדים")
- Neighborhood/city parenting groups ("הורים ב[שם עיר]")
- Professional-parent crossover groups (kindergarten teacher communities, SLP communities — relevant for Tier B creator relationships, not general seeding)
- "מה זה נורמלי" style groups where parents ask developmental questions

### Facebook seeding approach

Facebook groups allow longer posts than WhatsApp and are indexed by Google — posts here also have SEO value for the group's keywords. The format here is slightly more narrative than WhatsApp.

**Post template FB-1 — the honest experience post (for ambassador use in groups they already belong to):**

> שאלה שאני שואלת הרבה: האם זה נורמלי שהוא...?
>
> מצאתי שיש אפליקציה שנותנת לי תשובה שמבוססת עליו — לא על "כל ילד." שמה ארבור. היא זוכרת מה תיעדתי השבוע ומשם עונה.
>
> אני לא מנסה למכור שום דבר — זה פשוט עובד אצלי. אם מישהי רוצה לנסות — השאירי לי הודעה.
>
> [תגובה ראשונה: arborparentingapp.com — ניסיון חינמי]

**Post template FB-2 — the Rhythm moment (share-card as the post image):**

Post the Rhythm "I was right" share card (from creative asset `creative/arbor-rhythm-wasright-sharecard-he-v1.png`) as the post image, with this caption:

> כבר שבוע שאני מתעדת בארבור — ובערב קיבלתי: "צפוי שהשעה 17:00 תהיה קשה." הכנתי את עצמי. הכנתי את הילד. זה אפילו עבד.
>
> מי שמכיר/ה אפליקציות כאלה — שנה/י לי תגובה?
>
> [תגובה ראשונה: arborparentingapp.com — לניסיון חינמי]

**Post template FB-3 — reactive: answering a group question (organic seeding):**

Monitor the target groups for parents asking developmental questions Arbor directly addresses. The ambassador (or in a future state, an owned account) answers the question first with genuine information, then mentions Arbor.

> [Full, genuine answer to the parent's question — 3–5 lines of actual useful advice]
>
> אגב — יש אפליקציה שנקראת ארבור שעזרה לי לגשת לשאלות כאלה בצורה יותר מסודרת. היא זוכרת את הלוג של הילד שלי ועונה על פיו. קישור בתגובה אם מעניין.

**Hard rules for FB mega-group seeding:**
- Read the group rules before any post. Groups that ban commercial links require value-first posts where the link appears only in a comment and only if someone asks.
- Never post the same template verbatim in more than one group. Rewrite for each.
- Never claim to be a satisfied customer in a group where you have not genuinely used the product. Ambassadors use Arbor first; seeding follows experience.
- Do not post in more than 2 groups per week per ambassador during Week 1. Let signal accumulate before expanding.

## UGC challenge brief — #הגיבורשלי / #ArborHero

**Challenge name:** #הגיבורשלי (my hero) / #ArborHero (EN)
**Mechanic:** parents generate their child's Arbor avatar → share it on IG/TikTok with the hashtag. The child is the hero of their own story — the avatar is the visual, never a real face.

**Seeding script (for creators and ambassadors to seed the challenge, W1):**

Arbor's owned account (W1-01 IG Reel, this queue) launches the challenge. Creators and ambassadors who have received the app and generated their child's avatar seed the hashtag organically on the same day or within 48 hours.

Each seeding post must include:
- The child's avatar (synthetic — not a real photo)
- The hashtag #הגיבורשלי and #ArborHero
- @arborchilddevelopment tag
- A genuine one-line caption about their child (real experience — never a script)
- The referral link in the first comment (once P0-2 is live; omit until then)

The challenge does not require a "challenge" framing in the caption — it is simply the hashtag. Organic posts without campaign language travel further.
