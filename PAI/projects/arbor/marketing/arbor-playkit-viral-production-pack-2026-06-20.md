## Creator Brief

Ask creators to record one authentic parent moment, then cut to the Arbor PlayKit world.

Script:

> "היה לי רגע כזה של 'זה נורמלי?' במקום לפתוח עוד עשרה טאבים, ניסיתי את Arbor. הוא לא איבחן ולא הלחיץ. הוא פשוט סידר לי מה קורה ומה עושים הערב."

Shot list:

- Face-to-camera: 2 seconds, real parent moment.
- Screen/graphic insert: one generated Arbor PlayKit asset.
- End card: `arbor-playkit-video-end-card.png`.
- CTA: `נסו בחינם`.

Creator must avoid:

- Diagnosis claims.
- "This solved my child."
- Before/after cure framing.
- Medical emergency scenarios.
- Showing real private child data.

## Prompt Template For More Images

```text
Create a social marketing image for Arbor in the supplied Arbor PlayKit design language.

Subject: <specific campaign moment>
Format: <9:16 / 1:1 / 4:5 / 16:9>
Text: no readable text; leave clean empty zones for deterministic Hebrew copy.
Avoid: photorealistic lifestyle, dark clinical look, generic AI chatbot imagery, garbled Hebrew, diagnosis/cure claims, copyrighted characters.
```

## Production Notes

- Use the generated images as plates, then add final Hebrew/English copy manually.
- For Reels/TikTok/Shorts, keep the first overlay under 4 words.
- End every piece with a low-friction CTA: `התחלה חינם`.
- Main URL should remain the real domain once purchased; avoid using the `web.app` URL in viral posts.
- This pack supersedes the photorealistic image direction from `assets/viral-2026-06-19/`.
