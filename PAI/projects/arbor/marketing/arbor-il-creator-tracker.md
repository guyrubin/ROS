## Who we want (profile)
Micro over macro — engagement + trust beats reach at this budget. Prioritize:
- **Parenting/mom & dad creators** (HE) with 5k–80k, high comment-to-like ratio, real "day in the life with kids" content.
- **Adjacent professionals** punching above follower count: speech therapists (קלינאיות תקשורת), kindergarten teachers (גננות), child psychologists, occupational therapists, sleep consultants (יועצות שינה), parent coaches (מאמני הורים).
- **Community admins** of large WhatsApp/Facebook parent groups (different play — partnership, not a post).

Avoid: pure lifestyle/fashion with no parenting niche; anyone whose comments are bot-spam; macro-influencers we can't afford.

## Sourcing method
1. Instagram/TikTok hashtag mining: `#אמהות` `#הורות` `#אמאשל` `#גננת` `#קלינאיתתקשורת` `#הורים` `#אבאשל` `#ילדים` `#חינוך`.
2. "Following" graph of 2–3 known IL parenting accounts → their micro-peers.
3. Facebook parent mega-groups → identify active expert contributors.
4. Google/YouTube: HE parenting creators + SLP/psychologist content channels.
5. Score each on **engagement rate** (not follower count) + audience fit + brand-safety.
