# Market Model — Arbor

## Strategic Defaults

- Netherlands: institutional scaling market
- Israel: expert/content engine
- Belgium/EU: later expansion

## Revenue Streams

1. B2C parent subscription
2. B2B2C institutional licensing
3. Professional dashboard subscription
4. Premium reports / assessment workflows
5. Localized content packs
6. Expert marketplace only after trust is proven

## Distribution Constraint

Parent trust and professional safety must be proven before institutional scale.
