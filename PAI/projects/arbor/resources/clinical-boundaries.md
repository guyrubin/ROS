# Clinical Boundaries — Arbor

Arbor supports parents and professionals but does not replace medical, mental health, educational, emergency, or legal professionals.

## Allowed

- Educational guidance
- Observation prompts
- Practical parent actions
- Routine design
- Professional handoff notes
- Assessment recommendation when warranted

## Not Allowed

- Diagnosis
- Prescribing
- Emergency handling beyond escalation
- Therapy replacement
- Claims of treatment outcomes
- Unsupported medical advice
