## The goal, stated honestly
**Light a self-feeding viral loop in the Israel beachhead within 30 days — proven with real numbers, not vanity.**

"Viral in a month" translated through our own honesty gate (G1): a pre-launch product with no brand domain cannot *guarantee* sustained organic K≥1 in 30 days, and we will not claim it. What we **can** commit to and prove in 30 days is **ignition**: the loop is wired and instrumented, seeded in the densest IL parent channels, and measured climbing toward self-sustaining. Sustained K≥1 virality is the 60–90 day outcome this sprint earns. Anyone who promises guaranteed virality on a fixed date is selling — we measure instead.

## Strategy in one paragraph
Israel-first, organic-led, product-as-the-loop. The child is the **acquisition** angle (the shareable hero artifact); the parent is the **paying, retained** customer (the convergence: six surfaces on one record). Spend only to (a) wire the loop, (b) seed 15–25 micro-creators, (c) amplify what's already working. The densest real engine is **WhatsApp class-groups + IL Facebook parent mega-groups**, not paid ads. Every asset is essence-true (passes the Arbor Bar) or it doesn't ship — "generic" is how you *don't* go viral.

## Kill / scale criteria (read each loop cycle)
- **Scale** a creative/channel when its share→install rate beats the cohort average — push paid behind it.
- **Kill** any channel under the activation floor (installs that don't reach Double-Aha) — installs without retention are vanity; stop feeding them.
- **Honesty stop (G1):** if measured K underperforms, re-frame and pause spend asks — never paper over with a blended-K claim.
