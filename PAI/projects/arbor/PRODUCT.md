# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Three audiences, sequenced parents-first:

- **Parents** (primary, B2C) of children 0–12, often reaching for help late at night, mid-problem, asking "is this normal, and what do I do tonight?" Beachhead: Netherlands and Israel.
- **Professionals** (trust engine): coaches, speech & language therapists, child psychologists, OTs, educators who want longitudinal context and a clean handoff.
- **Institutions** (scale engine): municipalities, schools, daycare networks, insurers funding family support at population scale.

## Product Purpose

Arbor is a developmental operating system for families. It gives a parent a calm read on what's happening, a concrete plan for today, and a private record of their child that gets richer every time they use it. The moat is longitudinal child memory plus expert-reviewed knowledge, not the underlying model. Success: parents return because the record is theirs and compounds; professionals and institutions trust it enough to distribute it.

## Brand Personality

Calm, direct, humane, practical, structured, non-alarmist, high-agency. The voice of a senior parenting advisor who has seen this before and will not panic. Clinical credibility without coldness. Never ideological, never preachy, never fear-selling.

## Anti-references

- The cream-and-rainbow consumer-app default (the older `arbor-marketing-landing-page.html` lane): friendly but generic, drifts from the real product UI.
- Alarmist parenting content that sells fear.
- Chatbot-wrapper landing pages with a generic AI hero and no product substance.
- The SaaS hero-metric template, identical icon-card grids, eyebrow-on-every-section scaffolding.

## Design Principles

1. **Show the real product.** The page should look like the shipping app, not a separate brand world. Identity-preservation over reinvention.
2. **Diagrams carry the explanation.** Capabilities are explained through custom visuals and product mockups, not adjectives.
3. **Calm is the differentiator.** Composure, white space, and restraint signal trust; loudness would undercut the clinical promise.
4. **Memory is the spine.** Every capability connects back to the child-memory core; the page is built around that one idea.
5. **Earn the professional's trust.** Non-diagnostic, expert-reviewed, parent-owned data stated plainly, not buried.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA: body text ≥4.5:1, interactive diagram nodes keyboard-operable with visible focus, all motion gated behind `prefers-reduced-motion`, content never gated on JS (the interactive capability map is an enhancement over fully static capability content).

## Visual System (committed identity, from the shipping app)

- **Brand:** pine green `oklch(0.47 0.072 156)` with its ramp; deep pine for drenched sections.
- **Canvas:** cool, faintly-green off-white `oklch(0.985 0.004 168)` (deliberately not cream).
- **Ink:** deep green-gray `oklch(0.26 0.018 165)`, never pure black.
- **Type:** Fraunces (display serif) + Inter (body). Kept as the product's real identity even though Fraunces is a common default; identity-preservation wins.
- **Functional accents:** amber / blue / red from the product's semantic ramp, used only to mean something in diagrams (risk levels, states).
