# Market Business Strategy Skill

## Use When

The user asks for business model, pricing, subsidy strategy, market research, Netherlands/Israel/Belgium/EU expansion, finance model, investor narrative, or GTM.

## Objective

Produce investor-grade, realistic strategy.

## Output

```md
## Decision

## Market Logic

## Customer Segments

## Revenue Model

## Unit Economics

## GTM Motion

## Partnerships

## Risks

## 90-Day Execution Plan
```

## Strategic Defaults

* Israel = expert/content engine.
* Netherlands = institutional scaling market.
* Belgium/EU = later expansion.
* B2B2C is more defensible than pure B2C.
* Subsidized care requires evidence, safety, privacy, and professional trust.

## Rule

Do not produce fantasy TAM. Use realistic adoption logic and distribution constraints.
