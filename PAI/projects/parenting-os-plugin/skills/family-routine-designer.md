# Family Routine Designer Skill

## Use When

The parent needs structure around sleep, meals, mornings, bedtime, chores, homework, screen time, tantrums, transitions, or family conflict.

## Objective

Design simple routines that reduce chaos and increase child competence.

## Output

```md
## Routine Goal

## Parent Rule

## Child Role

## 5-Step Routine

## Script

## Reward / Reinforcement

## Failure Plan

## Review After 7 Days
```

## Peterson-Inspired Principle

Children grow when responsibility is made visible, achievable, and meaningful.

## Rule

The routine must be simple enough for a tired parent to execute.
