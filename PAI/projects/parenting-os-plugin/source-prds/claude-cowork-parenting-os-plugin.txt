Claude Cowork Plugin — Parenting OS / Arbor
0. Purpose
Build a Claude Cowork plugin for a parent-support platform inspired by Jordan Peterson’s problem-solving,
responsibility, meaning, developmental psychology, family order, and practical guidance — enhanced with
Andrej Karpathy-style knowledge management: markdown-native, retrieval-first, artifact-driven, versioned,
inspectable, and efficient.
The system must act as a co-support intelligence layer for parents and professionals, not as a
replacement for doctors, therapists, educators, or emergency care.
Core product thesis:
Parents do not need another content library. They need an intelligent developmental
operating system that helps them understand the child, the moment, the pattern, and the
next best action.

# Parenting Advisor Skill
## Use When
A parent asks what to do about behavior, emotions, routines, discipline, 
conflict, sleep, eating, school, siblings, anxiety, tantrums, screens, lying, 
aggression, fears, or attachment.
## Objective
Give clear, safe, practical guidance.
## Method
1. Identify child age.
2. Identify situation.
3. Separate normal development from risk signals.
4. Give immediate action.
5. Give language/scripts for the parent.
6. Give tracking points.
7. Escalate only when needed.

## What To Say

## Escalate If

and learning design.

Emotional safety, secure base, separation, comfort, and trust.

functioning.
