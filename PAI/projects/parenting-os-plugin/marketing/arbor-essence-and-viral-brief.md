# Arbor — Essence, Competitive Edge & Viral Brief (master)

**Created:** 2026-06-17 · **Grounded in the actual app** (`app/src/index.css`, Practice Studio / Hero Arcade, the competitive analysis). This is the source of truth every asset (video, image, landing, ad, copy) must trace back to. If a creative doesn't feel like *this*, it's wrong.

---

## 1. The real Arbor design DNA (match the app, not a generic "calm app")

Arbor has **two surfaces with one soul.**

### Parent surface — a warm "developmental field-notebook"
Not cold clinical, not blue SaaS. A beautiful, calm, hand-kept journal of *your* child.
- **Canvas:** soft sage-paper `#eef2ef`, white elevated cards `#ffffff`, recessed wells `#f4f8f5` / `#e9eeea`.
- **Ink:** soft slate-black `#29333f` (never pure black, never navy), muted `#5f6b75`.
- **Brand green ("clay"):** emerald `#34b277` / deep `#2a9c66`, mint chip `#e4f4ec`, green-ink `#1f8a5a`.
- **Warm accent hues** (used to mean something, calm not loud): peach `#d9763f`, lavender `#7a6bd8`, yellow/ochre `#c2882a`, pink `#d65f87`, sky `#3f8cc9`.
- **Type:** **Fraunces** (characterful display serif — the warmth) + **Nunito** (rounded humane sans). HE: **Frank Ruhl Libre** + **Heebo**.
- **Feel:** soft slate-tinted shadows, 10–22px radii, gentle fade-up motion. Composed, warm, premium, human.

### Child surface — PlayKit comic/game-world (scoped `.arbor-play`)
Joyful and designed, never the generic primary-color kids'-game look.
- **Sprout** — the friendly leaf mascot (gentle bob + sparkle), the child's guide.
- **HeroAvatar** — the child's *own* stylized avatar, embedded **as the hero** of comics and game-worlds.
- Big rounded forms (26–32px), **ink-outline + halftone** comic styling, themed worlds, warm celebration.
- Sits on top of the calm parent world — kids get delight, parents keep the premium journal.

### Anti-references (this is what made my earlier drafts wrong)
❌ cold teal/navy "clinical" blue · ❌ dark pine green · ❌ Inter/Assistant-only flat SaaS · ❌ rainbow-pastel kiddie · ❌ generic AI glowing-brain/chatbot · ❌ alarmist fear imagery. **Yes:** warm sage paper, emerald clay, Fraunces, the comic Hero world.

## 2. The moat — one sentence

> **Arbor remembers *your* child.** It builds a private, longitudinal developmental record — behaviors, moods, milestones, what you've tried, what works — and everything else gets smarter because it's built on that record. Competitors have content; Arbor has *your child's story.*

---

## 3. Arbor vs the field (the added value, named)

| Competitor | Their hook | **Arbor's win (only possible because of the memory)** |
|---|---|---|
| **Kinedu / Lovevery** | 2,000 activities, age-based | **Daily Play** picks today's activity by **age + your child's actual logged behavior** — they personalize by age *only* |
| **Huckleberry** (SweetSpot) | predicts nap timing | **Rhythm** predicts your child's **whole-day** friction / calm / focus windows from *your* logs — not sleep-only, not generic tables |
| **Cleo / Cooper / Maven** | a real expert behind the app | **Ask a Specialist** opens the human expert with your child's **full context packet** — their experts start cold |
| **BabyMind / Glow / Cudo** | AI tracker / cry analysis | Arbor's record is **anticipatory and actionable**, not a passive log |
| **All of them** | one feature each | Arbor is the **operating system** where memory → Rhythm → Play → Practice → Specialist **compound in one loop** |

**The line:** *"Every parenting app gives you content. Arbor gets to know your kid."*

## 4. The viral engine — each differentiator is a share moment

Virality here isn't a gimmick bolted on; it's the **real features being inherently shareable.**

| Differentiator | The viral artifact | Why it spreads |
|---|---|---|
| **Hero Avatar / Hero Comics** *(built)* | your kid as a **comic superhero**, their live avatar, shareable comic panels | parents *love* showing their kid; it's a delightful toy + identity → IG/TikTok gold. **The flagship loop.** |
| **Rhythm** | "Arbor predicted my kid's 5pm meltdown — and it was right" screenshot | uncanny, talkable, proof the app *knows* your child |
| **Daily Play** | a 10-sec clip of the kid doing today's household-item activity | UGC-native, "look what we did," low-friction |
| **Growth memory** | a beautiful monthly **Growth Card / "year of my child"** from the record | the field-notebook made shareable; emotional, recurring |
| **Ask a Specialist** | "the expert already knew everything about my child" story | trust proof, word-of-mouth |
| **The 2am Test** | "stop fear-Googling your kid" emotional hook | the ad wedge that pulls people in |

**Loop:** share artifact → friend installs → referral (free Plus month on activation) → their own artifacts. Seed with IL micro-creators + WhatsApp/FB parent groups. K-factor measured (instrumentation shipped).

## 5. What this means for creative (the correction)

Every video / image / page must look and feel like §1:
- **Palette:** sage paper + emerald clay + warm accents. **Not** teal/navy, **not** pine.
- **Type on assets:** Fraunces headlines + Nunito (HE: Frank Ruhl Libre + Heebo).
- **Parent moments:** warm, real, journal-like calm — soft daylight, the beautiful record on screen.
- **Child capability videos:** show the **real PlayKit world** — Sprout, the child's **HeroAvatar as the comic hero**, ink-outline/halftone game-worlds, genuine delight + the "what this builds" payoff. Lead with **Hero Comics/Arcade** (the flagship shareable), then Speech Coach / Mimic Studio / Feelings Lab / Daily Play / Adventures.
- **Always pair delight with the differentiator** — e.g. the comic clip captioned "Arbor turns practice into your kid's own superhero story," the Daily Play clip captioned "the activity that actually knows my kid."
