# Arbor — Gemini Prompt Pack (images + video)

**Created:** 2026-06-17 · For generating on-brand marketing assets in Gemini (Imagen / Nano Banana for images, Veo for video).
**How to use:** copy a prompt block verbatim into Gemini. For consistency, also **upload a reference image** (the Arvo avatar PNG or a brand frame) and say "match this style / character."

---

## 0. The essence of Arbor (read once — it's baked into every prompt below)

> Arbor is the **calm second opinion for parents** — it tells you what's going on with your child and what to do tonight, and it *remembers*. The feeling is a **senior, warm developmental advisor who has seen this before and will not panic.**

**Brand world — always:**
- **Light, airy, premium, clinical-humanist** (think a high-end health brand / קופת חולים פרימיום), never dark, never cutesy.
- **Palette:** cool near-white canvas `#F2F6F8`; pure white cards with soft blue-grey shadows; deep **navy ink** text `#0E1A2B`; **teal-green** primary `#19A88C`; **blue** accent `#2E5BD6`; warm off-white `#F3EFE6`. Functional red `#C0453B` ONLY for the "worry/fear" beat.
- **Motif:** a small leafy tree / sprout (the "Arbor" mark), calm rounded forms.
- **People:** real, warm, diverse parents and young children (ages 0–8), natural soft light, candid not stock-posed.
- **Mood:** composed, reassuring, honest, high-agency.

**Never (negative prompt for every asset):** dark pine-green backgrounds, cream-and-rainbow pastels, alarmist or fear imagery, clinical/cold hospital look, cartoonish kawaii, generic AI "glowing brain/chatbot" tropes, neon, heavy gradients, stocky fake smiles, busy clutter, watermarks, distorted hands/faces, garbled text.

**Campaign it serves:** *"The 2am Test"* — the enemy is fear-Googling your child at 2am; Arbor is the calm answer. Emotional arc when relevant: **dark, anxious night → blooms into a bright, calm, light world.**

> ⚠️ **Text-in-image limit:** Imagen/Veo render real words unreliably (especially Hebrew). Generate these **text-free** and add the headline/captions afterward in Canva/CapCut. Each prompt below is written to leave clean space for that.

## 3. Quick guidance for Gemini
- **Aspect ratios:** social/Reels `9:16`, feed `1:1` or `4:5`, web/YouTube `16:9`.
- **Consistency:** upload a reference image (Arvo PNG, or your best generated frame) and ask Gemini to "match this style/character/lighting."
- **Iterate:** if it drifts dark or cutesy, re-paste the §0 "Never" line and add "lighter, calmer, more premium and editorial."
- **Text:** generate clean/empty, add real HE/EN copy in Canva/CapCut (pull headlines from `arbor-launch-copy-pack.md`).
- **Diversity:** vary the parent/child in 1a/1c/2c across a few generations so the campaign feels real.

**Essence checklist before you ship any asset:** Light? Calm? Premium? Real people, not stocky? Teal-green + navy, not pine or rainbow? Non-alarmist? Leaves room to breathe? → if yes, it's Arbor.
