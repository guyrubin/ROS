# Arbor — Gemini/Veo Video Prompts: Children's Capabilities

**Created:** 2026-06-17 · Generate short promo/demo videos for Arbor's child-facing features.
**Workflow:** for each prompt, **attach the matching app visual** (screenshot or screen-recording of that feature) in Gemini, then paste the prompt. Veo will animate a child using *that* screen.

---

## 0. Child-layer essence (baked into every prompt)

> Arbor's child features (PlayKit) are **playful and joyful, but calm and premium — never loud, flashy, or overstimulating.** A warm, well-made kids' experience a thoughtful parent trusts. The companion is **Sprout**, a friendly little leaf/tree character.

**Always:** a happy, natural young child (age 2–8) genuinely engaged; a real, bright, tidy home in soft daylight; the device screen showing the **attached Arbor visual**; warm authentic delight (a smile, focus, a small "I did it!"); a calm parent sometimes nearby. Palette around them stays light, cool-white + warm wood + soft teal-green. Premium, editorial, candid.

**Never (negative prompt):** dark or pine-green backgrounds, neon, frantic motion, loud cartoon chaos, overstimulating flashing, stocky fake smiles, screens that look like generic kids' games, distorted hands/faces, garbled text, watermarks, more than one device, clutter.

**Format default:** vertical `9:16`, 5–8 seconds, gentle handheld camera, soft natural light, no baked-in text or music (add captions/VO/music in CapCut after). Stitch clips into a reel.

> ⚠️ Veo won't perfectly reproduce your UI on the screen — attaching the visual gets it close; for a pixel-perfect screen, film/animate the device dark and composite your screen-recording on top in CapCut.

## Build tips
- **Stitch a 20–30s sizzle:** opener → 3–4 capability clips → closer; add captions (pull from `arbor-launch-copy-pack.md`) + soft music in CapCut.
- **Per-feature Reels:** use one capability clip + a hook caption (e.g. "ask your kid to make a silly face 👀" for Mimic Studio).
- **Consistency:** reuse the same child/home across a set so it feels like one family; attach the Arvo image whenever Sprout/avatar appears.
- **Essence check before shipping:** child genuinely happy? light + calm, not loud? premium, not generic kids'-game? screen on-brand? → if yes, it's Arbor.
