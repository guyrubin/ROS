# Arbor — YouTube Strategy

**Created:** 2026-06-17 · **Verdict:** Shorts now (free), long-form evergreen later (lean). Never bespoke-produce on this budget.

---

## The split that matters
YouTube is two products under one roof. Treat them differently.

| | **Shorts** | **Long-form** |
|---|---|---|
| Role | Discovery / viral engine (like TikTok/Reels) | Evergreen authority + SEO/AEO library |
| Cost | ~€0 — repurpose vertical Reels we already make | Production-heavy → budget risk |
| Speed to installs | Fast | Slow (months to compound) |
| When | **Ignition (Phase 1) — now** | **Phase 2+**, lean |

**Why a channel is worth it at all:** YouTube is the #2 search engine and one of the most-cited sources by ChatGPT/Gemini/Perplexity. A calm "is this normal?" library becomes a durable AEO asset that recommends Arbor long after a Reel dies. But that's a compounding *later* bet — it must not pull budget or focus from the loop now.

---

## Phase 1 (now) — Shorts only, zero new production
- Create the channel ("Arbor — calm parenting"), HE-first; banner/avatar from the brand kit (pine green, wordmark).
- **Cross-post every vertical asset** (the 10 hooks, #ArborAvatar challenge, creator UGC) to Shorts. Same file, new surface.
- Link in description + channel → landing (`/il`). Pinned comment with referral/app link.
- Cost: editing time already counted in P0-7. Net-new spend: €0.

## Phase 2 (Weeks 9+) — lean evergreen long-form
- **Repurpose, don't invent:** turn the highest-traffic guide-hub articles + winning answer cards into 3–6 min talking-head/screen-record explainers. The script already exists as the article.
- Target long-tail search intent ("is it normal that my 3-year-old…", "toddler won't sleep what to do"). Each video = one query Arbor should own.
- Format: calm, face-to-camera or product-screen, end card → app. Batch-record to keep cost near-zero.
- Localize to NL/EN as those markets open, winners only.

## What NOT to do
- ❌ No high-budget produced series, no studio, no bespoke animation. It will silently eat the €10k.
- ❌ Don't make long-form the ignition channel — it's too slow for an Israel-first viral sprint.
- ❌ Don't gate the channel behind perfection; ship Shorts now from existing assets.

## KPIs
- Shorts: views → CTR to landing → installs (UTM-tagged links).
- Long-form (Phase 2): search impressions, watch-through, and **AI-assistant citations** for HE/EN parenting queries.
