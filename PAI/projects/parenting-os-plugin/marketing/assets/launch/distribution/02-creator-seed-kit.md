## Tier definitions and target profile

### Tier A — Parenting coaches and family creators (HE, 30–80K)
Profile: Israeli parenting coaches, "day in the life with kids" creators, mothers/fathers with genuine community engagement. Engagement rate matters more than follower count. Priority: accounts where comments are conversations, not emojis.

Sourcing: the current wave-1 candidates from `arbor-il-creator-tracker.md` (Tier A section: @galtamari1, @beitna.official for organic gift consideration). Add from Modash modash.io/find-influencers/israel/family and infludata.com/rankings/top-20-influencer-israel-family-instagram.

Deliverable ask: 1 hero Reel or feed post (own experience + their child's avatar) + 2 Stories + 1 group seed (they introduce Arbor in a WhatsApp group or FB group they're active in naturally).

Fee structure (L4 — do not state in DM): €40–60 per confirmed creator + free Family plan for one year. Wave-1 total cash for 5 Tier A creators: ~€250 (L4, confirm before any commitment).

### Tier B — Professionals with trust authority (SLP, peds, parent coaches, sleep consultants, 5K–30K)
Profile: Speech-language pathologists (קלינאיות תקשורת), child psychologists, occupational therapists, kindergarten teachers (גננות), sleep consultants (יועצות שינה), parent coaches (מאמני הורים). These accounts often punch above their follower count in trust. Their audience is exactly the ICP for the Ask Arbor and Practice Studio surfaces.

Sourcing: tracker Tier B section (Noa Barak SLP, Merav Nanus SLP, Shiri Marcus Lax SLP, Michal Dalyot). Add from FB parent mega-groups (identify the expert contributors who answer questions — not to pitch them in the group, but to find their profile and DM separately).

Deliverable ask: 1 honest Reel or feed post (their professional view on tracking children's development) + 1 Story.

Fee structure (L4): €20–35 per confirmed professional creator + free Plus plan for 6 months. Wave-1 total cash for 10 Tier B creators: ~€275 (L4, confirm before any commitment).

### Tier C — Nano-creators and engaged community parents (2K–8K)
Profile: Parents with small but highly engaged followings who genuinely use the app and post organically. Product-only, no cash fee.

Deliverable ask: 1 honest post about their experience, no script required.

Fee structure: free Plus plan for 3 months. No cash.

## Do/Don't guide for creators

### DO

- Share your **real** experience with your own child. The most powerful content is a genuine moment: a 5pm meltdown Arbor predicted, a question you asked at 2am that finally got an answer based on *your* child.
- Show the **real product UI** — the Rhythm day-bar, the answer card, the avatar, the Growth Card. Not a staged scene.
- Use your own words. This is not a script. We don't want it to sound like an ad.
- Post **your child's Arbor avatar** with #הגיבורשלי — the stylized character, never a real photo of your child's face.
- Place the referral link (arborparentingapp.com/join?ref=YOURCODE — live after P0-2 ships) in your **first comment** or Story link, not in the opening of the caption on a group seed.
- Tag @arborchilddevelopment in the post.

### DON'T

- Never make diagnostic or clinical claims: "Arbor diagnosed," "Arbor detected," "clinically proven," "recommended by pediatricians" — any of these are off-limits.
- Never state effect sizes: "improved my child's speech," "boosted development," "solved sleep problems permanently."
- Never use a real child's face or voice as the main marketing payload — the avatar is the shareable artifact, not a photo of your child.
- Never imply that Arbor replaces a pediatrician, speech therapist, or any other professional.
- Never use fear-bait hooks: "Don't miss the warning signs," "What parents don't know about their child," "Are you failing your child?"
- Never post the same copy you received from us verbatim. It will read as an ad and lose trust.
- Never post about Arbor in a WhatsApp group or FB group by leading with a link in the opening message. Value first — the link goes in the first comment or after you've answered a question organically.
