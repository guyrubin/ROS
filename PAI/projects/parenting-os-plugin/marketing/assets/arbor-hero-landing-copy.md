---

### 2. MAGNET PROOF BLOCK

Upload one photo. Arbor builds a comic where your child is the hero — the mission, the style, the powers are shaped by who they are. It takes under 90 seconds.

At the same time, Arbor asks you one question. You answer it; Arbor responds with something specific — not generic developmental content, but a response that holds your actual child in mind. Parents who've used it describe it as the first time an app felt like it knew their kid.

The comic goes straight to WhatsApp. The answer stays in your private record.

---

### 3. PARENT VALUE LADDER

**Rhythm**
Arbor learns your child's real patterns day by day — by the end of the first week you have a daily window that shows you when they're at their best and when they need space. Not a prediction. A pattern built from your real logs.

**Daily Play**
One activity each day, chosen against what Arbor already knows about your child — not average-age content, but something calibrated to their actual logs and history.

**The 2am Answer**
When something is worrying you and you don't know if it warrants a call — ask Arbor. It knows your child's history and responds to your specific situation, not to a generic developmental question.

**The Record**
Every observation, every answer, every note compounds into a private file that belongs entirely to you. It doesn't reset, it doesn't disappear, and it isn't shared with anyone.
