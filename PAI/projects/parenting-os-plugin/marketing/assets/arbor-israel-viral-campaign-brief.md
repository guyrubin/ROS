# Arbor Israel — 30-Day Viral Campaign Brief (ECD)

**By:** `arbor-brand` (ECD) · 2026-06-21 · Anchors: [GOAL-viral-30d.md](../../mesh/marketing/GOAL-viral-30d.md) · [BRAND-STRATEGY.md](../../mesh/marketing/BRAND-STRATEGY.md). Production calendar: [arbor-israel-viral-content-calendar-30d.md](arbor-israel-viral-content-calendar-30d.md).

## The big idea — "הילד שלך — ביד יציבה" / "Your Child, Steady"
**Organizing thought:** *Every other parenting app meets your child once and forgets them. Arbor is the only thing that was there the whole time.* Not a feature campaign — an identity claim. Make the enemy (parenting amnesia / the 2am fear-Google / the five-app graveyard) viscerally recognizable to every Israeli parent, then answer with one truth: **Arbor זוכר — Arbor remembers.** In WhatsApp group culture "remembers" is the unlock: parents share what makes another parent say *"זה בדיוק אני."*

## The breakout creative (delivers G-4 / >100k views) — "שנה שלמה" / "The Whole Year"
60s vertical (clippable to 15s for WhatsApp), Reels + TikTok IL.
- **Hook (0–3s):** dark room, phone at 2:17am, a hand types *"האם זה נורמלי ש…"* → the bar fills with ten million strangers' results → hard cut to white: **"הם לא מכירים את הילד שלך."** Recognition, not a jump scare.
- **Turn (10–40s):** the screen becomes the real Arbor record (field-notebook surface) — months animate up; an Ask-a-Specialist session opens and the expert's first line already names the child's logged 5pm friction pattern. The record does the talking.
- **Payoff (40–55s):** the Growth Card renders the child's HeroAvatar as the hero of the year; real-parent VO: *"לא ידעתי שזה מצטבר. שמישהו שומר. עכשיו אני רואה שנה שלמה שלה — וזה שלי."* End card: **Arbor. ביד יציבה.**
- **Share trigger:** the 2am hook = "tag your partner, זה אנחנו"; the Growth Card = "I want to make one of those." The video creates desire for the artifact; the artifact is the loop's payload.
- **Only-Arbor (one-word-swap):** the expert-opens-knowing and Growth-Card-from-real-logs moments are structurally impossible on any competitor that opens cold and has no record.
- **Production:** one real IL parent (not a celebrity); real Arbor UI, never mockups/AI illustrations; no child's real face (HeroAvatar is the representation); understated acoustic; Frank Ruhl Libre display + Heebo body. **arbor-safety pre-clears the Rhythm "based on your logs" framing — bands, no minutes, no causal claim.**

## The hero share artifact — the monthly Growth Card
The loop payload. A 9:16 card rendered monthly from the child's real record: the HeroAvatar in the comic world, three plainly-stated logged moments (positive/neutral only — never a flag), one Rhythm insight, one defining line. Shared as an image straight to WhatsApp/Stories — no app, no account to view. **Safety line on the generation screen:** *"הכרטיס שלך, הנתונים שלך"* — no real face ever, first name opt-in only, C2PA/SynthID signed. The Arbor badge deep-links `/join?ref=` so every share is an instrumented loop entry (needs Week-0 domain + referral wiring).

## Three supporting concepts
- **A — "חמישה אפים, ילד אחד" / Five Apps, One Child:** kills the five-app graveyard; the convergence table on one record.
- **B — "המומחה שכבר מכיר" / The Expert Who Already Knows:** kills the cold-start expert; two opening moments side by side.
- **C — "#הגיבורשלי / #ArborHero:** the avatar as a participation loop — the share is the product experience (a stylized hero, never data). Week-1 ignition spark; the Growth Card is the monthly retention flywheel.

## Narrative arc
W1 **name the enemy** (recognition, nothing sold yet) → W2 **ignite the loop** ("The Whole Year" + Growth Card + #הגיבורשלי, proof = the product working) → W3 **amplify what earned it** (paid only behind proven creative; press on the privacy/memory moat) → W4 **compound + read** (second Growth-Card cycle; publish the honest K read + 60-day path).

## ECD guardrails for the sprint (do-not-ship rules)
1. **Name the enemy, or kill it** — no generic "parenting is hard" frame.
2. **Show the real record, or the convergence is just a claim** — real screenshots, never feature illustrations.
3. **No child's real face/behavioral data as the viral payload** — HeroAvatar only; patterns not flags; arbor-safety on anything that blurs this.
4. **Calm is load-bearing** — recognition, never urgency theater; if it raises the heart rate it fails the 11pm test.
