Capabilities, in detail

What each one actually does for you.

Ask Arbor

Describe the moment you are in. Arbor turns it into a calm, structured answer instead of a wall of generic tips.

  • A clear read on what is likely happening.
  • A risk level: low, medium, high, or escalate now.
  • Three things to try tonight, and what to watch.
From a messy moment to a plan in one reply.

Child Intelligence

Every milestone, note and observation in one place, with the patterns connected. Arbor links today's question to last month's answer.

  • Milestones and behaviour over time, not in isolation.
  • A Weekly Insight that surfaces what changed.
  • Patterns flagged as observations, never as labels.
Three small notes become one clear pattern.

Growth Plans

Advice you can't keep is useless. Arbor turns a read into a routine your family can actually run: bedtime ladders, morning resets, behaviour plans.

  • Step-by-step ladders, not a vague goal.
  • Templates for the situations that recur.
  • Resets for when a hard week knocks it off track.
Small, repeatable steps the whole house can follow.

Practice Studio

Short daily play for speech and development, scored gently by domain. It reports bands you can act on, never an age-equivalent label.

  • Speech, language, motor, social and thinking, tracked as bands.
  • Daily missions and adventures that keep it a game.
  • Camera and audio stay on the device. Always.
Five domains, shown as bands, not a single scary number.

Care Network

When it is time to involve a professional, Arbor writes the handoff for you. The coach, therapist or teacher starts from the full picture, not a blank form.

  • A versioned brief: history, patterns, what you tried.
  • You choose exactly what is shared, and with whom.
  • Export to PDF, or share securely inside Arbor.
Nothing is shared until you choose to share it.

How it works

From "help, what now?" to a plan, in three steps.

Ask in plain words

Type the situation the way you would tell a friend. No forms, no checklists, no jargon. "She bites at daycare." "He won't eat anything green."

Get a read and a plan

Arbor answers in the same shape every time: what is happening, how serious it is, what to do tonight, and what to track. Calm, specific, doable.

It remembers

The exchange is saved to your child's record. Next time, Arbor already knows this week, so the advice keeps getting sharper and more yours.

Why Arbor is different

The more you use it, the more it becomes truly yours.

A new chatbot can answer a question. It cannot be your child's history. Every note, milestone and "what worked" compounds into a record that no competitor can recreate, because they were not there for the last six months. You were, and so was Arbor.

What we will not do

Four promises, held in the product.

These are not a marketing footer. They are enforced in how Arbor is built, because a tool that holds your child's history has to earn that place.

Non-diagnostic by design

Arbor explains patterns and points you to what to observe. It never labels or diagnoses your child, and it tells you plainly when to see someone who can.

Expert-reviewed where it counts

The high-stakes guidance, safety, mental health, anything sensitive, is reviewed by clinicians, not generated and forgotten.

Your data, your child

You own the memory ledger. Export it or delete it, in full, whenever you want. GDPR is built in, and personal details are redacted before any model sees them.

Calm, not alarmist

Direct, practical, high-agency. Arbor is built to lower the temperature in the room, never to sell you fear about your own kid.
