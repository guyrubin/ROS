# Arbor Viral Engine — Operating Playbook

**The method the three viral pods run.** Owner: `arbor-marketing-lead`. Read with [MESH.md](MESH.md) (roster) + [OPERATING-MODEL.md](OPERATING-MODEL.md) (the cycle) + [BRAND-STRATEGY.md](BRAND-STRATEGY.md) (the Arbor Bar). Backlog of live work: [MARKETING-BACKLOG.md](MARKETING-BACKLOG.md) §8.

> **Why this exists.** "Viral level" is not a creative-quality problem — it's **volume-of-shots × hook-discipline × distribution**, fed by **trend intelligence**. The team that breaks out separates four jobs the mesh used to blur: *intelligence* (what works now), *creative production* (the watchable asset), *distribution* (getting it to humans), and *loop* (the read). This playbook wires them. Evidenced against Duolingo + Cal AI short-form playbooks (2025–26); IL/WhatsApp specifics flagged as under-sourced below.

---

## The org, in one line
`arbor-insights` (trend brief) → `arbor-content` (hook/caption first) + `arbor-brand` (taste) → `arbor-creative` (asset + variants) → `arbor-distribution` (owned queue + creators + group seeding) → `arbor-acquisition` (K-factor read) → `arbor-critic-market` (eval) → back to `arbor-insights`. Small, trend-fed, autonomous to T2 — that autonomy is the most-cited reason a tiny team out-ships a big one.

## The weekly viral loop (one breakout-hunt sprint, repeatable)
1. **INTEL brief** (`arbor-insights`, Fri) — rising IL-parent sounds/formats scored by **growth-stage not volume**; the **rule of three** (≥3 niche creators on one audio/format in 24–48h → flag); a refreshed micro-creator shortlist; top FB-group pain clusters; last week's post-mortem. A late flag is worthless — surface inside the **48h window**.
2. **BRAINSTORM** (lead + brand + content) — conviction-gated, not consensus. Pick the week's concepts from the brief.
3. **HOOK FIRST** (`arbor-content`) — write the caption/pin line + 5–10 native-Hebrew hook variants per concept. Copy leads; creative serves the line.
4. **PRODUCE IN BATCH** (`arbor-creative`) — one session = a week of volume. Mix ≈ **50% UGC/scenario-skit · 25% clinician talking-head (HeyGen) · 25% animated child-as-hero**, text-on-screen layered across all.
5. **GATE** — Arbor Bar self-check → `arbor-brand` ECD craft veto → `arbor-safety` (no real child face/voice/data; no clinical/effect-size claim).
6. **DISTRIBUTE** (`arbor-distribution`) — publish 5–7/wk to the owned queue (autonomous post-gate); **draft** creator packs + WhatsApp/FB group seeds (L3 — never sent unconfirmed); stay in comments 15+ min mining the next beat.
7. **READ + ITERATE** (`arbor-acquisition` + `arbor-insights`) — next morning read 3-sec retention, watch-through, **saves/shares** (TikTok now reweights saves 2–3×). Kill <40% intro-retention. For any post beating baseline, re-cut its winning hook into 3–5 new variants and re-ship. The breakout is usually a derivative of a mid-performer, not a fresh idea.

## Thresholds & numbers (the discipline)
| Lever | Target | Source-confidence |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Intro (3-sec) retention | **>70%** hold; **<40% = kill** | High (multi-source) |
| Posting volume (30-day push) | **5–7/wk**, never <3 | High — cadence = more breakout lottery tickets |
| Concept → amplify ratio | ~8–10 concepts → 1 worth amplifying; ~1 genuine breakout per 20–40 shots | Medium (directional) |
| Micro-creator tier | **10K–25K** (ER peaks here; collapses by 50K) | High |
| Creator outreach math | ~500 messaged → ~50 reply → ~10 post | Medium (single-sourced, directional) |
| Trend freshness | use a sound within **48h** of emergence (~3–5× push) | High |

## Toolchain (validate on free tiers; upgrade once a format proves)
**HeyGen** (script→talking-avatar, no filming; video-translation with preserved lip-sync — record EN once, ship HE) → **CapCut** (edit, captions, trending-sound sync; no watermark) → **Canva** (graphics, thumbnails, text-on-screen, brand-kit) → **phone** for raw UGC (the authenticity layer no AI tool replaces). Re-confirm pricing/limits at execution — they shift quarterly.

## The animated child-as-hero rule (legal-safe AND high-performing)
- **Marketing creative uses ONLY a generic, customizable hero character — never rendered from a real child's photo, biometric, or likeness.** Under the 2025 amended COPPA rule, a generic avatar on its own is **not** "personal information"; an avatar generated from a real child's image, combined with other data, **is** — and triggers parental-consent. Stay cleanly outside that trap. (Distinct from the in-app avatar, which may be face-derived — the marketing surface must use the generic hero regardless.)
- **Keep the register parent-facing.** Cartoon avatar + high-pitched narration are the exact cues the FTC/ASA use to deem content *child-directed*. The audience is the **parent** — calm clinician-mentor voice, the hero is the child's on-screen proxy, never content aimed at kids. This protects the ad-placement/targeting position. `arbor-safety` co-owns this gate.

## Honest gaps (validate before trusting)
- **IL WhatsApp class-group + FB parent-mega-group seeding mechanics, and HE-RTL listening tooling, are under-sourced** in the open literature — the community layer here is designed-by-inference. **Action:** before committing distribution budget, run a primary-source pass (interview 2–3 IL parenting micro-creators + audit live IL FB parent mega-groups). Closed WhatsApp groups are **not** API-listenable — `arbor-insights` relies on ambassador field-notes, not scraping.
- Cal AI retainer figures (~150 creators × 4/mo) are single-sourced — directional only. The *structural* pattern (high-throughput creator-seeding as a distinct function) is multi-sourced and sound.

## Guardrails (inherit the mesh envelope)
Publishing to **owned organic surfaces** is autonomous after the gate. **Always gated:** paid spend / creator fees (L4, state amounts) · outbound DMs/email to real people (L3) · clinical/effect-size claims (`arbor-safety` veto) · real child face/voice/data · domain/store/product-code. The ECD vetoes anything generic — "generic" is precisely how you *don't* go viral.
