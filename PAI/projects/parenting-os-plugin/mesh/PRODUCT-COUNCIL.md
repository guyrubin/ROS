# Product Council — the one intake

**Version:** 1.0 · **Created:** 2026-06-21
**Owner:** PAI (product). **Mandate:** turn five input streams into **one scored `PRODUCT-BACKLOG.md`** the Orchestrator executes. Decides *what* gets built and *in what order*; never builds, merges, or deploys.

The council is how a real product company decides. It convenes the [Head of Product](teams/product.md), the [Advisory Board](teams/advisory.md), the [Marketing mesh](marketing/MESH.md), and the [CIL](improvement/CIL.md), reconciles their inputs, applies the clinical gate, scores, and hands a ranked, build-ready queue to [`arbor-orchestrator`](ORCHESTRATOR.md).

---

## 1. The five input streams

| Stream | Source agent | Brings | Tag |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **Product** | `arbor-product` | Discovery briefs + PRDs + a success metric per candidate; the voice-of-parent (forward user research) | `product` |
| **Advisory** | `arbor-advisor` | Worth-building rubric scores + reframes (competence/order/responsibility/truth/meaning) | `philosophy` |
| **Clinical** | `arbor-clinical-lead` | Clinical requirements (what we *should* build to be sound) + the soundness/claim gate on everything else | `clinical` |
| **Marketing** | `arbor-marketing-lead` | Feature-requests from competitor gaps + demand signal (the funnel hands these over per its DoD) | `demand` |
| **Improvement** | `arbor-evaluator` (CIL) | Verified findings + themes from the live-app critic panel | `cil` |

> PAI roadmap intent and CoS portfolio constraints sit **above** the council and set its priorities; founder ([FOUNDER]) items enter directly at top priority.

## 2. The council loop (`/arbor-product-council`, weekly)

```
 SENSE ─────────► REVIEW ──────────► SCORE ──────────► WRITE ──────────► HANDOFF
 gather the 4    advisor rubric +    rank by the      candidate queue   top safe, build-ready
 streams         clinical gate       formula below    in PRODUCT-       items flagged for the
 (parallel)      on each candidate   (top ~12)        BACKLOG.md         Orchestrator's next wave
```

- **SENSE** — pull the five streams (the product + marketing + CIL backlogs already exist on disk; advisory + clinical are dispatched live).
- **REVIEW** — `arbor-advisor` scores each candidate on the rubric; `arbor-clinical-lead` runs the clinical gate on anything touching development/behavior/speech/health/claims.
- **SCORE** — compute priority; dedupe across streams (one root cause, not ten symptoms).
- **WRITE** — append a dated **`## Council Intake — <date>`** candidate block to `PRODUCT-BACKLOG.md`. The council **proposes**; it does not rewrite the canonical body. A human or the Orchestrator promotes candidates into the live tracks.
- **HANDOFF** — flag the top `safe` + `build-ready` items for the Orchestrator; surface `gated` and `[FOUNDER]` items as an explicit decision list.

## 3. Scoring

```
priority = (reach × impact × confidence × strategic_fit) ÷ effort
```
- **strategic_fit** folds in the advisor rubric (aligned > tension > off-rubric) and PAI roadmap intent — a high-engagement, off-rubric idea is *down-weighted on purpose*.
- **confidence** drops for thin demand signal or contested clinical evidence.
- Ties break toward the moat (longitudinal child record) and toward retention (the known constraint).

## 4. riskClass + the clinical gate (binding)

Every candidate carries a `riskClass`:
- **`safe`** — no claim, no child-data/billing/cost/consent surface. Auto-eligible for a build wave.
- **`gated`** — carries a developmental/medical/effect-size claim, or touches safety/consent/billing/cost/child-data, or surfaces the philosophy adviser's identity. **Never auto-built.** Filed for human decision.

**Clinical gate:** a candidate touching development/behavior/speech/health/claims **cannot be marked `build-ready`** until `arbor-clinical-lead` returns `soundness: pass` and `claims: none|substantiated`. A soundness VETO or an unsubstantiated claim holds the item, exactly like an `arbor-safety` veto.

(3) **Default to invite-your-own-provider** before any marketplace; the marketplace is the fallback, not the front door (AAP medical-home continuity). (4) **Packet = the product, booking = the wrapper:** per-session, scoped, time-boxed, revocable consent framed as the parent granting access they own (a single checkbox is not sufficient consent); success metric = *session produced a parent-owned plan / fewer repeat consults over time*, NOT consults-booked / rebooking / 15%-of-exports. (5) **Graduation stance** over a subscription-to-consults loop. (6) Firewall §0/§3 p11 — all booking + packet copy "developmentally informed," never "clinically validated"/"clinician-reviewed," and carries no adviser/thinker identity.
- **Still gated by procedure regardless:** the two hardest gates in the product co-exist here — **billing** (Stripe session payment, Level 4) and the **deepest third-party child-data egress** (full memory packet to an external party, COPPA/GDPR) — both require Guy + `arbor-safety` sign-off independent of the clinical hold.

CHARTER §3 principle 11 (no clinician-endorsement claim).
- **Verdict:** soundness:concerns / claims:UNSUBSTANTIATED / riskClass:gated → **HELD**. Cannot be build-ready until the "richer-than-telehealth" claim is dropped, the provider-identity firewall + invite-your-own-provider default + packet-not-booking metric are in, and `arbor-safety` clears billing + child-data egress. ## 6. Rules
1. **One backlog.** The council is the *only* legitimate birthplace of a build item. Pods and loops draw from `PRODUCT-BACKLOG.md`; they do not invent scope.
2. **Propose, don't clobber.** The loop appends a dated candidate block; it never edits the canonical backlog body. Promotion is a deliberate step.
3. **The clinical gate is binding.** No claim-bearing item is build-ready without Clinical Board sign-off.
4. **Advisory is weighting, not gating.** The rubric shifts priority; it never blocks.
5. **Gated never auto-builds.** `gated` and `[FOUNDER]` items are surfaced for Guy, not scheduled.
6. **Every cycle ends in memory** — append a one-liner to [MEMORY.md](MEMORY.md) and `/PAI/MEMORY.md`: streams pulled, candidates written, top build-ready, gated decisions surfaced.
