## Concept note (message, not words)

**The insight that stops the scroll:** Every other parenting app gives you content about children in general. Arbor gives you your child — a private, compounding record that gets smarter every time you use it. That is not a feature. It is a structural difference no incumbent can retrofit.

**Hero line:** "Every parenting app gives you content. Arbor actually knows your kid."

**What every field must earn:** the parent must feel, by the end of a single field, that Arbor is different in kind — not in quantity of features — because it *remembers their child*. The enemy is parenting amnesia and the five-app graveyard. The voice is a calm senior clinician-mentor who ends with something actionable.
