## 1. Decision

Build Arbor into **the parent-mediated, evidence-grounded, provenance-clean, parent-owns-the-data AI for raising a kid — explicitly NOT a kid-facing companion.** Win on **retention** (the category's binding constraint: 12–18%/mo churn as children age out of stages) by making the longitudinal memory moat compound monthly, wrapped in AADC-clean moat-native growth loops, and protected by trust-as-product that converts the 2025–26 regulatory backlash into Arbor's primary GTM weapon.

## 2. Why it wins

- **Structural position no incumbent can copy:** Good Inside coaches only the parent; Heeyo/companions only entertain the child (now radioactive — FTC inquiry, 44-AG letter, Character.AI ban, Fairplay AI-Toys advisory); Kinedu/BabySparks only list activities; Nanit only senses via hardware. Arbor closes the full loop **child play → developmental signal → parent guidance** in software, on a parent-owned memory record.
- **The moat already ships:** append-only, parent-approved longitudinal child-memory that every feature reads/writes. Compounding it monthly is the retention answer competitors structurally lack.
- **Trust is the wedge, not overhead:** for a kid-adjacent AI in 2026, "the AI for raising your kid you can actually trust" (provenance-signed, parent-controlled data, no open companion) is positioning regulators are actively forcing — and that no current leader can claim.

## 3. The three north stars (product pillars)

1. **VIRAL** — moat-native growth loops, measured honestly (installs-per-sharing-parent-per-month; **no blended-K promises** — Gate G1). Lead loops: Second-Guardian Invite (1:1, two-sided, adult-to-adult), Milestone Share Card, WhatsApp-first (IL), two-sided referral, safe stylized hero artifact. The child's real face/data is **never** the viral payload.
2. **CAPABLE** — best-in-market depth on the loop no rival closes: Parent Real-Time Coach (100% cited, talks to the parent never the child), child-as-hero story/comic studio, bounded (non-companion) kid voice, zero-effort longitudinal tracking, real child-phoneme ASR.
3. **TRANSFORMING** — evidence-based outcome change + retention: narrativized memory moat ("The Story of [Child]"), tiny-habits/implementation-intentions, parental self-efficacy/reflective-functioning, fresh-start re-engagement, guided human-touch tier, and an internal Evidence Engine. **Cite the mechanism, never transfer the clinical effect size to user copy until Arbor has its own data** (Gate G2).
4. **TRUST-as-moat (cross-cutting)** — image-gen cost cap, C2PA+SynthID provenance, Family Trust Center, no-open-companion guardrail, age assurance/VPC, red-team + safety-eval + abuse-reporting.

## 6. Governance gates (binding on every item)

- **G1 — Loop-math honesty:** measure installs-per-sharing-parent-per-month with the full factor chain; no blended-K guarantees.
- **G2 — Copy governance:** mechanism-cited, clinical effect-size never transferred to user-facing surfaces until Arbor's own measured data (T6) exists; outcome verbs banned until then.
- **Non-negotiables:** no open kid companion; no dark patterns/guilt aimed at minors (incl. parent-facing streak guilt — infinite catch-up, "moments logged"); COPPA-2026 biometrics & retention; privacy-first / parent = named data controller; default external-share omits child render + per-share explicit consent + ephemeral reference-photo handling (auditable invariant); surveillance-not-diagnosis on tracking (EU MDR read before any clinician-facing artifact); safety-critical/red-flag coach responses never metered/upsold; all generated assets C2PA+SynthID signed; standing red-team + safety-eval + abuse-reporting before scaling any AI surface.

## 7. Risks

- **Cost leak (live):** image-gen has no cost cap — financial + abuse risk; blocks all generative/viral features. **First thing fixed (S2).**
- **Regulatory overreach:** transformation claims without G2 → unsubstantiated-health-claim exposure. Gate enforced in build.
- **Concurrent-session conflict:** multiple sessions/agents touch prod; uncoordinated deploys can clobber hosting. Mitigated by the autonomy charter's coordination protocol.
- **Over-parallelized L-bets:** small team — force-rank to one L-bet per quarter (start C1).
